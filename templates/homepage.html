Information about URLs


/scrapnews;

Will scrap news around the world.

/scraptechnews;

Will scrap technology news.

/savenews;

Saves scrapped data to MySQL db.

/savetech;

Saves scrapped data to MySQL db.

/news;

Shows data from DB.

/technews;

Shows data from DB.

/delnews;

Delete all news from db.

/deltechnews;

Delete all tech. news from db.

/search;

You can search any news from both news and technology news table.
